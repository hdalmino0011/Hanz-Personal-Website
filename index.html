About the Developer

Hanz is a Web Developer and System Engineer with a passion for crafting digital experiences that are as functional as they are beautiful. His background spans frontend and backend development, UI/UX design, hardware systems, and customer service — an unusually broad vantage point on how technology actually serves the people using it.

Educated at Cebu Technological University in the Bachelor of Science in Information Technology program, he continuously sharpens his skills across the full product lifecycle — from writing code, to designing interfaces, to understanding the hardware running beneath it all.

"Outside of coding, design tools like Photoshop, Illustrator and After Effects keep the technical honest — and the creative disciplined."

That dual fluency — equally comfortable in a terminal or a design file — shapes everything that follows in this dispatch.
